The Spiritual Root

The Support of Ancestry

An organic community is a web of families and decades-old friendships. When a marriage experiences strain, this network provides a "large degree of support." For those born into this light, the community acts as a protective vessel for the soul's growth.

The Individual Mission

The newcomer who breaks ties with their own family often finds themselves in a vacuum. Without the "token help" of their new group, they must rely on their own individual connection to the Divine. The refusal of a community to fully accept an outsider is often not an act of malice, but an innate intuition. As the world moves toward a global state, the contraction of tribal identities becomes more intense.

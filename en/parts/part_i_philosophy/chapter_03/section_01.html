  • Rewarding Good / Punishing Evil: This reveals God's Oneness by sifting out the bad.
  • The Method of Chaos: Slight variations in events cause infinite chaos, yet this chaos ultimately aligns to reveal Oneness in the Next World.

The Divine Lack

Man was created to express God's Goodness. Paradoxically, before creation, there was a "lack" in the Divine Traits‚Äîthe lack of revelation. As Hegel suggested, each trait implies its opposite. As long as a trait is not being used, it lacks the quality of being.

"To correct the 'deficits' of Goodness and Wisdom, God created the Circular Sephirot (surrounding) and the Straight Sephirot (filling)."

The Nature of Oneness

The idea that "God is One" means there is one Creator and one Cause.

What Oneness Does NOT Mean

  • It does not mean "one quality" or "one quantity" (which would imply divisibility/definition).
  • It means that nothing exists besides Him; everything derives its existence constantly from Him.
  • This refutes the Aristotelian idea that "existence" is a separate property shared by things. God is Existence.

The Paradox of the Saint

The religious level has a unique paradox: the deeper you dive, the more you encounter contradictions. One might find that deep study of the Gra or Rabbi Nachman leads to self-contradictions. These are not circles, but a Spiral leading to the next higher level. Just as the rising sun makes everything bright before the sun itself is visible, the light of Divine knowledge fills the world even before the final redemption is evident to all.

"By the time the sun really is ready to rise, it is already so bright that it is almost irrelevant if you can see the sun or not. When the whole world will be filled with the brightness and glory of God, then we will know the Messiah is near."

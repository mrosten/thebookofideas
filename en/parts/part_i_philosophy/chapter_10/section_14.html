The root of Physics and Math is in the worlds which are above the world of Emanation, and thus at the time of the breaking of the vessels they fell into the lower worlds. But if one learns them with fear of God, they have the power to bring one up to his higher root (This is the reason the sciences have been emphasized by the non-Jews who are from the same world).

The idea of learning science and math is to be attached to the thoughts of God in the creation of this world. This is what the Sages of the Talmud call the Work of Creation. The idea of learning Kabbalah is to be attached to the thoughts of God in the creation of the higher spiritual worlds. So, one fulfills the commandment of being attached to God by learning science, math, and kabbalah. By learning Jewish law, one attaches his mind to the tree of knowledge of good and evil which was forbidden to Adam, and by doing so he fell from the world of formation into the world of action . However, for us here one is required to know the law.

So, it is safe to assume that social minded people that are battling for the good of society, not for personal honor are from the spiritual Israel of Formation. But on all these levels everything depends on one's intent so if one is involved in contemplation of God with some ulterior motive he is not from the spiritual Israel, even though they are from Creation.

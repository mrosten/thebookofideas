The Strategy of Decision

The Four Pillars of Decision Making

When faced with the unknown, one must have a strategy.

  1. Know Yourself: Understand your lack and your strength. As the Greeks said, "Know Thyself."
  2. Reconnaissance: Like Col. Simons, never go in blind. "Find Out."
  3. Situational Awareness: Like a fighter pilot, be aware of the whole sphere, not just the front.
  4. Active Waiting: Putting oneself in God's hands does not mean sleeping; it means doing nothing until the clear will of God appears.

Chapter 4: Piety ‚Äî Section VII

Prophecy and the Crown

The Brit Menuchah mentions that Prophecy comes from the Tzitz of the Cohen Gadol, which is representative of the sefirah of the Crown (Keter). It comes only through the path of judgment.

‚ö° The RLC Circuit of Prophecy

LInductance (Keter)
CCapacitance (Vessel)

The Crown prevents the mind from reaching the Infinite Light directly. By hitting against this constraint, vessels are formed to receive the Light.

This helps to answer Nietzsche's objection to the saints of old that afflicted themselves to come to spiritual levels. Rabbi Nachman also mentions in Likutei Moharan (Vol. 1, Ch. 24) that the crown prevents the mind from reaching the infinite light, and thus by hitting against the constraint of the crown, vessels are made to receive the light.

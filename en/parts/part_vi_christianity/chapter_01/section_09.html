The Divine Spark in the Physical

One of the theological hurdles regarding the Trinity is the assertion that God cannot descend into a physical body. Yet, the Gemara (Sanhedrin 95b) presents a startling narrative regarding Sancheriv. Rabbi Abahu states: "If the verse didn't write it, it would be impossible to say." The text implies that the Holy One, Blessed is He, appeared as an old man to give Sancheriv a haircut, leading to a spark singeing his beard.

Rabbi Abahu emphasizes that this is not a mere parable; the language used‚Äî"The Holy One, Blessed is He"‚Äîtypically refers to the Sephira of Tiferet (Beauty/Truth). This implies a direct manifestation of the Divine into the physical realm, a concept echoed in the Patriarchal narratives.

The Four Diadochi

Continuing the analysis of Daniel's prophecy regarding the fracture of Alexander's empire, historical fulfillment aligns with the four Diadochi (Successors):

  1. Egypt under Ptolemy Soter (South).
  2. The East (Indian territories/Afghanistan) given to Chandragupta Maurya.
  3. Syria/Babylon under the Seleucid Empire (North).
  4. Macedon/Greece under Cassander/Antigonus (West).

This historical fracturing implies that the subsequent verses refer to the latter period of the Greek Kingdom‚Äîspecifically the transition into Roman protection. Daniel 11 openly predicts a Messiah arising after the fall of the Greek empire, which aligns with the rise of Rome.

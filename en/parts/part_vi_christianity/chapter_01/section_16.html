The Shabbat Philosophy

Jesus taught: "The Shabbat was made for man." Critics called this heresy. Yet the Rambam wrote identically: the object of the Torah is the well-being of body and soul; specifically, Shabbat was given to witness Creation and provide rest and comfort (Moreh Nevuchim III:27, 43).

Many of life's important principles one has to accept from his arch enemy, or never get them at all. (Love as a principle was never noticed in Jewish writings. Jewish writings are devoid of it for about 2000 years. It's the Christians that made a big deal about it and finally we noticed it.) Some people are on the level of action, some on the level of words and some on the level of thought. The way of life which applies to one can't apply to the other. But within each group there is good and bad. On each level one must find God.

The attitude of being a fighter for justice or of hating any group or feeling like the victim of injustice or raising children to have these attitudes creates vindictive people who are in permanent conflict with the world and fighting for some cause. Sometimes in life one should realize he has to come to the highest point he can get to. Carl Jung felt that in the middle age period a transformation comes. The sages said forty is the age of understanding.

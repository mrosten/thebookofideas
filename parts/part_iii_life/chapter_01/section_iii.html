Maybe he would have made more progress if he had stuck with his wife and kids. Give your children a relationship, especially when they are teenagers. If you don't get along with them at that age, be rest assured, they will find someone else to get along with and fall into some stupid cult which will be their parent figure. Don't ignore religion and philosophy so your children who will search will be able to talk with you about it. Your children are your life. You receive flow only to the degree you give flow. In Akudim, Zeir and Nukbah are greater than Abba and Imma, so as we progress towards the final correction many times the children are better than the parents.

There is no such thing as children. Children are just adults that look small, and adults are children who look big. The only way to rebuke your children is if it hurts you more than it hurts them to do so. Children don't respond to words or deeds but to feelings. Feelings are all that matter in human relationships, therefore there are no rules. If a parent who has no love raises children according to all the rules, the result will not be good. If a parent with love raises his/her children even if he breaks all the rules, the result will be good. Giving a child in to the hands of a parent or guardian who doesn't love the child - is murder.

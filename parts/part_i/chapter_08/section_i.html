INTRODUCTION The redemption is one of the most painful subjects. All Israel expected the Messiah to come at the end of the second Temple period and deliver them from the Romans. This was correct to expect, since the prophets that predicted the destruction of the First Temple clearly imply that when the Second would be built that the Messiah would then come then, and that it would last forever. Well ‚Äî that prediction proved wrong. Then, after its destruction they were expecting Bar Kochba to deliver them ‚Äî that also didn't work. Then, we got an assortment of saints that tried to bring the Geulah themselves and all failed. Then, Chabad got into the Messiah business and in fact made a lot of money doing so.

Chapter 9: On Learning Torah

Section IV

Moharan , Sefer HaMidot [six middle sephirot] [Malchut]. [This is only to the Rambam and Chovot Halevavot ] This next diagram is a close up of physicality of beauty (zeir anpin): † [IMAGE: image004.gif] c: Crown. There is the root of the five books of Moses w: Wisdom k: Kindness. v: Victory. u: Understanding. p: Power. s: Splendor. In these right and left hand sephirot are located the roots of the prophets. k: knowledge b: beauty f: foundation In those middle three sephirot are located the roots of the sacred writings [like the psalms proverbs, etc]. The next diagram is a closeup of Formation of Royalty [IMAGE: image005.gif] The middle three “pbk” [power, beauty, kindness] are the root of the three parts of the Mishna Seeds , Times and Women . The lower three are the roots of the three other parts Damages , Sacrifices , and Purity

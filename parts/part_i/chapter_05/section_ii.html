Chapter 5: Happiness

Section II

where King Solomon praises happiness in one and puts it down in the other… It answers that happiness in God is a good thing, happiness not in God is not . The reason for this is that h appiness is klipat noga [the tree of knowledge of good and evil], so when it is in God it becomes included in holiness and the bad in it falls away. But depression is from the three totally evil klipot. Therefore any depression is evil. Now you could ask about Mozart and Beethoven or artists in general where it seems a bit of unhappiness stirred and inspired their best creations. On one level you can answer that their experiences were unhappy, not their attitude. Also , Rabbi Shneur Zalman explained that judgements come from a higher source than blessings so when one stays attached to God in the long run the judgements turn into the highest blessings.

When the next year he developed Matrix Mechanics [or quantum mechanics] the subject of resolution became a powerful tool to show the truth of the uncertainty principle.

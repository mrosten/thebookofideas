An old philosophy paper I wrote as a teenager: A. “To examine an object pedantically is not to think. It is an examination by a specific formula which has no bearing on the idea itself. It is examination from a different viewpoint than that of the idea's maker and cannot do (accomplish) anything. It is insensitivity to the idea. It is lack of understanding. [For example] to examine myself from an entirely psychological and rational point of view is not to understand myself.

The Gemara in Macot 24A says, ‘Rabbi Simlai expounded : 613 commandments were said to Moses...

[King] David came and established them on 11... Isaiah came and established them on 6... Micah came and established them on 3.... Isaiah then established them on 2... Amos came and established them on 1… Rashi writes, “[…] Established them on 11.” For, at first people were righteous and could bear the yoke of many commandments, but the later generations were not so righteous and if they would try to keep them all no one would merit. So, David came and established them on 11.” Rashi is clear. However, the other first authorities explain this Gemarah differently.

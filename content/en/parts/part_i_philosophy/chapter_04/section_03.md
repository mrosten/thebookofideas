<h4>The Eternal Mitzvot</h4>
<p>Rashi suggests that not all 613 commandments were meant for all generations. King David and the Prophets "established" them on fewer principles (11, 6, 3, 1) because people could no longer bear the full yoke without losing their awe of God.</p>

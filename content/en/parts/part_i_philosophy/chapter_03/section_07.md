<h3>The Reality of the Strange Loop</h3>

<p>My proof differs from Hegel's. Kant admitted Spinoza proved God <em>in the mind</em>. I simply ask: does the <strong>Law of Cause and Effect</strong> exist in reality? If yes, then God is proved in reality.</p>

<p>This is the <strong>Strange Loop</strong>: a progression from the physical universe to the spiritual one, until we reach infinite spirituality, which inevitably reconnects back to the physical universe.</p>

<blockquote class="fancy-quote">
    "Just as the system of natural numbers implies super-natural numbers, a complete system of logic implies a Super-Logic. This infinite recursion is the ladder to God."
</blockquote>

<h3>The Divine Lack</h3>

<p>Man was created to express God's Goodness. Paradoxically, before creation, there was a "lack" in the Divine Traits‚Äîthe lack of revelation. As Hegel suggested, each trait implies its opposite. As long as a trait is not being used, it lacks the quality of <em>being</em>.</p>

<blockquote class="fancy-quote">
    "To correct the 'deficits' of Goodness and Wisdom, God created the Circular Sephirot (surrounding) and the Straight Sephirot (filling)."
</blockquote>

<h3>The Nature of Oneness</h3>

<p>The idea that "God is One" means there is one Creator and one Cause.</p>

<div class="concept-box">
    <h4>What Oneness Does NOT Mean</h4>
    <ul class="clean-list">
        <li>It does <strong>not</strong> mean "one quality" or "one quantity" (which would imply divisibility/definition).</li>
        <li>It means that nothing exists besides Him; everything derives its existence constantly from Him.</li>
        <li>This refutes the Aristotelian idea that "existence" is a separate property shared by things. God <em>is</em> Existence.</li>
    </ul>
</div>

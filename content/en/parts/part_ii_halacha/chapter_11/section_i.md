The Gra, perceiving a danger to the integrity of the Law, excommunicated the followers of the Magid of Mezritsh, calling them "Klipat Noga"—a dangerous mixture of light and shadow. The Chassidim, specifically the Baal HaTanya, argued they were saving the soul of the people. This was an "Argument for the Sake of Heaven," a clash of Titans over the very definition of how to serve God.</p>

            <h4>The Virgin Birth Defense</h4>
            <p>Critics argue that Isaiah 7:14's <em>almah</em> means "young woman," not "virgin." Yet the Jewish commentary Ibn Ezra states: "It is probable that it means virgin <em>or</em> a girl who has had sex—either one is equally likely." The ambiguity is linguistic, not theological. Moreover, Genesis records that "the Sons of God came unto the daughters of man and they gave birth"—a precedent for divine-human procreation.</p>

            <h4>The Shabbat Philosophy</h4>
            <p>Jesus taught: "The Shabbat was made for man." Critics called this heresy. Yet the Rambam wrote identically: the object of the Torah is the well-being of body and soul; specifically, Shabbat was given to witness Creation and provide rest and comfort (Moreh Nevuchim III:27, 43).</p>

            <h4>Hora'at Sha'ah: Emergency Override</h4>
            <p>When Eliyahu sacrificed on Mount Carmel (a Torah prohibition), the Rambam explains he didn't receive special prophecy—he acted on the principle of <em>hora'at sha'ah</em> (temporary necessity). Couldn't Jesus's actions fall under this same principle, given the spiritual emergency of the time?</p>

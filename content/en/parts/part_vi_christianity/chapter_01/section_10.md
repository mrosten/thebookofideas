            <h4>The Recursive Hierarchy</h4>
            <p>The prophet Hosea states that God appeared to Jacob and served him (<em>Hosea 12:3-6</em>). While sages often attribute this to the "Angel of Esau," the deeper mystical truth is that the Divine includes all levels. If there "is nothing besides Him," then even the adversarial forces are maskings of the One. Paradoxically, if Jesus acts as the intercessor of Esau (Rome), he is functioning as a necessary bridge in the redemption process.</p>

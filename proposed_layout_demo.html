Typographical Number Theory

TNT (Typographical Number Theory) suggests that since the universe is analytic, it can be expanded into a Taylor Series. This provides a mystical proof for the necessity of marriage: a single point (an individual) has limited derivatives and cannot truly expand.

The circular sephirot are analytic—smooth, continuous, and infinite. The straight sephirot initially appear discrete, but through the "cylinder" of the worlds, they too participate in this analytic geometry.

<h4>The Savior's Requirement</h4>
<p>A savior's effectiveness is contingent upon the people's belief. Just as Moses could only save the small fraction of Israel that believed in him—leaving the rest to perish in the darkness of Egypt—Jesus could not fulfill the national promises of building the Temple or gathering the exiles because his own people rejected him. The failure to achieve the national messianic goals was not a failure of the savior, but a failure of the "Vessels" (the people) to receive the Light.
